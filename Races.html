Human

Humans are the most versatile and adaptable race; as well as, the largest and most well received race in Ravnica. Race Roll on Empathy and Athletics.Human Stunt - At character creation, you may take one additional regular stunt. You have the ability to go unnoticed in large crowds.

Elf

One of the oldest and wisest races of Ravnica, Elves have become skilled in both the arcane and martial arts over centuries of research and practice. Race Roll on Lore and Will. Elf Stunt - Once per scene, when you succeed with style on a defence roll with Athletics against Fight, you may force your opponent to attack another of your enemies in the same zone with the same attack roll. You have the ability to go unnoticed in large crowds.


Dwarf


The newcomers on the plane of Ravnica; said to have existed on the plane for only 1,000 years. Most dwarfs have found their home in the underground of Ravnica and are unparalleled in their ability to create grand mechanisms. Race Roll on Physique and Mechanics. Dwarf Stunt - You don’t ever have to spend a fate point to declare that you have the proper tools or materials for a particular job using Mechanics, even in extreme situations. You can see perfectly in the darkest of places and are not impeded by any amount of darkness.

Vedalken

Vedalkens are some of the most cunning and intelligent beings on the plane of Ravnica; expanding their knowledge to even surpass that of the Viashino. Race Roll on Lore and Empathy. Vedalken Stunt - Without rolling, you can tell if another non-PC is lying so long as the target’s Deceive is equal to or lower than your Empathy. In the case of high Deceive, you will know that you cannot tell, but will never receive a “false positive.” You have the ability to read at a faster rate than any other race.

Vampire

Vampires on the plane of Ravnica do not thirst for blood; rather, feed on the thoughts of others. Many Vampires hold higher places in society and have predominant roles in the businesses of Ravnica.Race Roll on Stealth and Provoke. Vampire Stunt - Once per refresh, you may roll Provoke in place of Investigate to tap into the mind of another to gather information to an opposed Will. You can see perfectly in the darkest of places and are not impeded by any amount of darkness.

Giant

Giants of Ravnica sport a hardy constitution and an overwhelming physical build. They are mostly barbaric in nature and do not hold a very high intelligence. Race Roll on Physique and Provoke. Giant Stunt - You can use Provoke to defend against Fight attacks, but only until the first time you’re dealt stress in a conflict. You can make your opponents hesitate to attack, but when someone shows them that you’re not unstoppable your advantage disappears. You have the ability to notice more from a greater distance given that nothing is obscuring your view.

Minotaur

Often misunderstood as slow and dim witted, Minotaurs on the plane of Ravnica are fierce, sharp-witted, and not easily taken down. Race Roll on Physique and Athletics. Minotaur Stunt - +2 to Physique rolls made to create advantages on an enemy by wrestling or grappling with them. Your Notice skill works unimpeded by conditions like total concealment, darkness, or other sensory impairments in situations where someone or something intends to harm you.

Centaur

Noble and peaceful protectors of the people. Centaurs hold a fierce loyalty to their own kind and will stop at nothing to defend what is morally right. Race Roll on Rapport and Athletics. Centaur Stunt - You work well together with a companion rider to defend yourselves. +2 to defend with Athletics while being ridden. Your Notice skill works unimpeded by conditions like total concealment, darkness, or other sensory impairments in situations where someone or something intends to harm you.

Rhox

An unstoppable force in combat; this proud race of rhinofolk served as Ravnica’s finest soldiers centuries ago and still serve the roll to this day. Race Roll on Physique and Empathy. Rhox Stunt - Once per scene, as a single action, you may move two zones in a straight line then make a physical attack. Your Notice skill works unimpeded by conditions like total concealment, darkness, or other sensory impairments in situations where someone or something intends to harm you.

Loxodon

The elephantfolk of Ravnica hold some of the plane’s greatest achievements on their backs; serving as mentors, politicians, and some of the finest archivists on the plane. Race Roll on Physique and Rapport. Loxodon Stunt - You may roll Physique to push yourself and an opponent into an adjacent zone, provided there are no situation aspects impeding movement. Your opponent may defend with either Athletics or Physique. Your Notice skill works unimpeded by conditions like total concealment, darkness, or other sensory impairments in situations where someone or something intends to harm you.

Merfolk

Merfolk call the open seas their home and serve to protect whatever body of water remains within the cityscape of Ravnica. They have the inherited ability to sense danger faster than any other race due to the poaching of their people for centuries. Race Roll on Deceive and Athletics. Merfolk Stunt - +2 to defend with Athletics for the first exchange after you become aware of a threat. You have the ability to breath underwater.

Dryad

Dryads are deeply connected to the “Worldsoul” of Mat’Selesnya. The smallest race on Ravnica, what remains of this peaceful people live among the remaining forest of Ravnica and a large majority find themselves aligned to the Selesnya guild. Race Roll on Rapport and Lore. Dryad Stunt - Your natural poise and grace enable you to pass quietly and undetected. You may use your Athletics skill in place of Stealth whenever silence alone would help you move undetected. You have the ability to go unnoticed in dense forest.

Faerie

The mischievous tricksters of Ravnica. Faerie’s role on the plane of Ravnica had changed dramatically after the Guildpact was put into effect. Race Roll on Deceive and Athletics. Faerie Stunt - +2 to overcome, attack, defend, or create advantage with Deceive, Burglary, and Stealth. Faeries may only ever have a maximum of 3 physical stress boxes no matter their Physique skill. -2 to overcome, attack, defend, or create advantage with Physique, Fight, and Provoke. You have the ability to notice more from a greater distance given that nothing is obscuring your view.

Weird

The newest race born to the plane of Ravnica. Created by one of Izzet’s finest alchemists not even 100 years ago, this race of people exist to serve anyone in need of assistance and love doing so. Most Ravnicans find this race to be foolish and naive, but what lies deeper within these elementals has yet to be truly discovered. Race Roll on Rapport and Empathy. Weird Stunt - Once per scene, you may identify that you have assisted a specific organization, guild, or individual in order to get your way and add +2 to a Contacts, Deceive, Provoke, or Rapport roll. You have the ability to breath underwater.

Goblin

The second largest race of Ravnica, Goblins have survived many hardships and are most often prepared with the proper weaponry. They are found among most all of the different guilds of Ravnica. Race Roll on Athletics and Mechanics Goblin Stunt - Once per conflict, you can force the opponent to use a mild consequence instead of a 2-point stress box on a successful Shoot, Destruction, or Fight attack. You have the ability to go unnoticed in large crowds.

Lich

Undead sorcerers of Ravnica that have succeeded in using their own conjuration magic into death. This necrotic race are the second smallest race of Ravnica; however, their numbers have been growing at an increased rate over the past few years. Race Roll on Lore and Deceive. Lich Stunt - When you place an aspect like Feigning Death on yourself, no-one can attack you while the aspect remains in play. You can see perfectly in the darkest of places and are not impeded by any amount of darkness.

Demon

Demons of Ravnica are one of the first races to exist on the plane. While most revel in causing destruction and pain, some have found new purpose beyond the actions of their evil minded ancestors. Race Roll on Provoke and Deceive. Demon Stunt - When you suffer a physical consequence, you can invoke that consequence for free on your next attack. If you suffer multiple physical consequences, you get a free invocation for each. You can see perfectly in the darkest of places and are not impeded by any amount of darkness.

Spirit

Spirits live within the shadows of Ravnica. Another of the smaller races, spirits have held positions among the Orzhov syndicate and Azorius senate for centuries. Few know how Spirits managed to exist on the plane of Ravnica, as known black magic only conjures undead Liches to live beyond death or conjure spirits for only a limited time. Race Roll on Stealth and Deceive. Spirit Stunt - +2 to create an advantage or overcome with Stealth whenever you are in near-darkness or total darkness. You can see perfectly in the darkest of places and are not impeded by any amount of darkness.

Angel

Known to be the first beings to exist on the plane of Ravnica. Angels find themselves in a position much like that of the Merfolk; constantly being hunted down and striped of their wings. As a result, many angels stay with their own kind, defending the plane and themselves together. Race Roll on Will and Empathy. Angel Stunt - You have the ability to fly to great distances and move two zones for free in a conflict without rolling, instead of one, provided there are no situation aspects restricting movement. You have the ability to notice more from a greater distance given that nothing is obscuring your view.

Viashino

Dragonkin; born from the efforts of Izzet’s guild leader Niv-Mizzet. One of, if not the most intelligent race on Ravnica. Highly skilled in the arcane and martial arts, few other races can compare to their brute strength and magnificent minds. Race Roll on Lore and Physique. Viashino Stunt - Once per scene, when you succeed with style on a create advantage roll with Physique and place an aspect on an opponent, you may exchange one of your free invocations for a 2-shift hit on that opponent. Your Notice skill works unimpeded by conditions like total concealment, darkness, or other sensory impairments in situations where someone or something intends to harm you.
