About the GM

Dennis Singmaster was born in 1992 in Philadelphia, PA. He is a dental hygienist with Children's Dental Health. In addition to his hygiene license from the Community College of Philadelphia, he also has a bechelor's degree in Science from Penn State Abington. His hobbies include playing Magic the Gathering (of course), video games, tabletop, and reading. His favorite color is orange and he has two cats. He has a Sister Ashley Adams, brother in law Rick Adams and a niece Victoria Adams.
